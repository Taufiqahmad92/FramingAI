The following information was released by the National League of Cities (NLC):

The National League of Cities (NLC) will host a press conference to spotlight some of the most pressing issues
local leaders from across the country are addressing, including how cities are utilizing AI, working to improve civil
discourse, advocating for federal disaster funding, and more. This event will also commemorate NLC's 100th
anniversary, reflecting on a century of impactful work empowering and uniting city leaders nationwide.

The press conference is part of NLC's City Summit conference, which will include more than 4,000 mayors, city
council members, and other local officials from across the country.

WHAT: NLC City Summit Press Conference

WHEN: Wednesday, November 13, 2024 1:20 PM EST

WHERE: Tampa Bay Convention Center, East Central Hall, Third Floor, Tampa, FL

Speakers:

Mayor Jane Castor, Tampa, FL

NLC President, Mayor David Sander, Rancho Cordova, CA

NLC Second Vice President, Mayor Steve Patterson, Athens, OH

NLC Immediate Past President, Mayor Victoria Woodards, Tacoma, WA

NLC CEO and Executive Director, Clarence Anthony
